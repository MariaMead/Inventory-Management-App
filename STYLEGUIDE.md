# Inventory Management App Style Guide

## Colour Palette

- Primary Colour: #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Grey)
- Secondary Colour: #127b7b (Dark Cyan)
- Accent Colour: #FFFFFF (White)

## Font Choices

- Montserrat (headings)
- Fira Sans
- Arial

### General Font Size

- 1 rem (16px)

### Heading Sizes

- H1: 2 rem (32pxpx)
- H2: 1.5 rem (24px)
- H3: 1.25 rem (20px)
